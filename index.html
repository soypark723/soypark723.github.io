QLOQ

QLOQ is a customizing clock brand which means unique clock. With the theme of optical art and light, WALL QLOQ and TABLE QLOQ suggest new concepts of clock to the user. QLOQ aims to provide product-service system, so the user can take his pick from diverse selection. At QLOQ’s webpage, the user can experience patterns and colors customization.

  • Client: none(coursework)
  • Date: Mar 2015 - Oct 2015
  • Category: Product Design

THE BRAILLE SERIES

Braille is a tactile writing system for people who are blind or visually impaired. By using braille, the user can read and write texts. Nevertheless, blind people still face lots of problems in daily life. In this project, we tried to solve some of these problems with a briaille motif, which is a familiar subject to visually handicapped people.

  • Client: none(coursework)
  • Date: Sep 2015 - Dec 2015
  • Category: Interaction Design

CHUBBY PIG

CHUBBY PIG is designed for the childeren who are paying no attention to saving. In reality, pigs gain weight by eating lots of food. We used this as a metaphor for the piggy bank. By feeding the CHUBBY PIG with coins, the user can fatten the piggy bank.

  • Client: none(coursework)
  • Date: Mar 2014- Jun 2014
  • Category: Interaction Design

OUCH!

OUCH! is designed to prevent people from stepping on the grass by personifying it. Even though there is a notice to keep out from lawn, people are often unaware of it and just step on the grass. OUCH! warns the user with the sound of ‘OUCH!’ whenever they step on the grass.

  • Client: none(coursework)
  • Date: Mar 2014 - Jun 2014
  • Category: Interaction Design
